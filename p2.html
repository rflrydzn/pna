P10-M worth of ‘shabu’ seized in anti-drug ops in Iloilo town

By Perla Lena

September 30, 2023, 4:16 pm


ILOILO CITY - In a recent anti-drug operation conducted by the Estancia Municipal Police Station in Iloilo province, two male suspects were apprehended. The police arrested an individual known as "Rene," 43, a resident of Novaliches, Quezon City, and his 22-year-old accomplice from Barangay Gogo. The operation, which took place in Barangay Tacbuyan around 6:10 a.m., resulted in the seizure of 38 sachets of suspected shabu, estimated to have a street value of PHP10.88 million. The arrested individuals were allegedly planning to deliver the 1.6 kg. supply to a buyer in Iloilo City later that morning. Alias Rene, originally from Barangay Pa-on, had been under police monitoring, having arrived from Manila with illegal items. Although Alias Rene has been on the drug watch list, he has no criminal record. The suspects underwent a drug test and are expected to face charges on Monday. The police commend the operation for successfully preventing the distribution of this substantial volume of illegal drugs.
