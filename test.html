Accessibility Instructions

Accessibility Features

We are committed to making our website accessible to everyone. Here are some features:

  • Text alternatives for non-text content.
  • Captions and alternatives for multimedia.
  • Readable and accessible fonts and colors.
  • Use TAB key to navigate links and buttons.
  • Use ENTER key to open a link.
  • Use SKIP TO CONTENT to go to your desired section.

Reporting Accessibility Problems

If you encounter any accessibility issues or have suggestions for improvement, please email us.
