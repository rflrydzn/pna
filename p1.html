Bicol police holds solidarity cycling event for peaceful, orderly BSKE

By Connie Calipay

September 30, 2023, 5:14 pm


LEGAZPI CITY - The Police Regional Office (PRO) 5 in Bicol expresses confidence in achieving secure, accurate, free, and fair Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan Elections (BSKE) through the collaboration of stakeholders across different provinces. PRO-5 organized a solidarity cycling event, drawing thousands of participants simultaneously across Police Provincial Offices (PPOs) in the region. Brig. Gen. Westrimundo Obinque, PRO-5 director, commended participants for promoting unity and cooperation, emphasizing the goal of ensuring a clean, peaceful, and safe election on October 30. The cycling event aimed to foster unity, promote health, and contribute to a more democratic future. Maria Aurea Bo-Bunao from the Commission on Elections (Comelec) also participated, highlighting the importance of collective efforts in the election process and urging vigilance against vote buying, which she termed the "cancer of democracy." A tree-planting activity and an integrity pledge by more than 800 BSKE candidates in Nabua, Camarines Sur, further underscored the commitment to violence-free and honest elections. Comelec-Bicol data reveal that the majority of villages in Camarines Sur are under the green category, indicating generally peaceful and orderly conditions.
