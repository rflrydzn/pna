Accessibility


  • Access Keys

    • Home: Press ALT + Q
    • About: Press ALT + W.
    • Subscription: Press ALT + A
    • Transparency: Press ALT + V
    • Contact: Press ALT + Z
    • Accessibility Information: Press ALT + C


    • National: Press ALT + ` (backtick)
    • Provincial: Press ALT + 1
    • Opinion: Press ALT + 2
    • Business: Press ALT + 3
    • Features: Press ALT + 4
    • Health: Press ALT + 5
    • Foreign: Press ALT + 6
    • Sports: Press ALT + 7
    • Travel: Press ALT + 8
    • Environment: Press ALT + 9
    • Scitech: Press ALT + 0

  • Accessibility Features

    • Text alternatives for non-text content.
    • Captions and alternatives for multimedia.
    • Readable and accessible fonts and colors.
    • Use TAB key to navigate links and buttons.
    • Use ENTER key to open a link.
    • Use SKIP TO CONTENT to go to your desired section.

If you encounter any accessibility issues or have suggestions for improvement, please email us.
